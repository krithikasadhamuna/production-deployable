=====================================
   CODEGREY AGENT - 2 MINUTE SETUP
=====================================

STEP 1: GET YOUR KEY
--------------------
1. Go to: https://dev.codegrey.ai
2. Login with your account
3. Click your name (top right) -> API Keys
4. Copy the key that starts with "usr-api-"

STEP 2: INSTALL
---------------
WINDOWS:
  - Extract codegrey-agent-windows.zip
  - Right-click INSTALL.bat -> Run as Administrator
  - Paste your key, press Enter

LINUX:
  - Extract codegrey-agent-linux.zip
  - Run: sudo ./install.sh
  - Paste your key, press Enter

MAC:
  - Extract codegrey-agent-macos.zip
  - Double-click install.command
  - Paste your key, press Enter

DONE!
-----
The agent will appear in your dashboard in 60 seconds.

Need help? Contact your IT administrator.
